# Privacy Policy

Withdrawal Portal helps Shopify merchants receive and manage online withdrawal declarations from their customers.

## Data Processed

The app processes:

- merchant shop domain
- order number and Shopify order ID
- order creation timestamp
- customer email address
- selected order line items and quantities
- withdrawal request timestamps
- request status and audit events
- transactional email delivery identifiers

## Purpose

The app uses this data to verify a customer's submitted order number and email address, show eligible order items, record the withdrawal request, notify the merchant, and send confirmation email to the customer.

The app does not sell personal data and does not use withdrawal request data for advertising, profiling, or unrelated analytics.

## Retention

The default retention period is three years. Merchants can request a different retention policy if their legal counsel requires it.

## Hosting And Subprocessors

The production app is designed to be hosted in Europe. Transactional emails may be sent through a merchant-configured provider such as Mailgun EU, SMTP, or Postmark.

## Shopify Privacy Webhooks

The app subscribes to Shopify compliance webhooks for customer data requests, customer redaction, shop redaction, app uninstallation, and scope updates.
